Intro

MyFinder is a an organized binder that is totally personalized to you. You can have and write down all your materials for classes, while keeping it all in one place. What's more, you can also manage your money and track your balances and usage. MyFinder is a must-have for all students!

Finance

Through the finance feature, you can now keep track of how much money you spent, your monthly allowances, and savings.

Binder

This is the main feature of MyFinder, where students can add classes and keep all their material in their respective tab. You can also use the todo list feature, where you can add assignments.
